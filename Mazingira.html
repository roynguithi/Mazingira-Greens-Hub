Our Tree Seedlings

Meru-oak (Vitex keniensis)

  • Environmental conditions: Grows in altitudes of 1200-2100m, Mean annual rainfall of 900-3000mm, Prefers deep sandy-loam soils
  • Botanical characteristics: Grows 12-30m tall, 3m diameter, has a thin bark, rough and slightly fissured
  • Price: 300 Kshs

Mango tree

  • Environmental conditions: A warm environment with ideal temperatures of 21°C
  • Botanical characteristics: Deep taproot and profuse surface roots, stout trunk (90cm in diameter) and an umbrella-shaped crown that may reach 20-40m high
  • Price: 500 Kshs

Avocado

  • Environmental conditions: Warm 60-80°F, humid climates of about 60-80% humidity
  • Botanical characteristics: Elliptic to egg-shaped leaves (4-12 inches), tall or spreading, small, greenish flowers borne in inflorescence
  • Price: 500 Kshs

Eucalyptus (Tasmanian blue gum)

  • Environmental conditions: 400-1200mm annual rainfall, free draining soils
  • Botanical characteristics: Leaves are long, slender and hang downwards, on a single stalk, flowers have fluffy white or red buttons
  • Price: 800 Kshs

Outeniqua yellowwood (Afrocarpus falcarpus)

  • Environmental conditions: Sandy-loam soils, Mean annual rainfall of 900-3000mm, Elevations of 1200-2100m above sea level
  • Botanical characteristics: Grows up to 25m tall, gray and often purplish bark, small, stiff lance-shaped leaves
  • Price: 600 Kshs

Iroko tree (Milicia excelsa)

  • Environmental conditions: Plenty of sunlight, well draining soils
  • Botanical characteristics: N/A
  • Price: 700 Kshs

Glaucum (Sequoiadendron giganteum)

  • Environmental conditions: Can tolerate reduced light levels to some extent, prefers deep, well-drained loamy soils, annual precipitation 900-1400mm
  • Botanical characteristics: Uniformly scale-like leaves, reddish-brown fibrous bark, Can grow to 83m and 31m circumference
  • Price: 400 Kshs

African tulip tree

  • Environmental conditions: Thrives in abundant sunlight, Requires plenty of moisture, Soil fertility and drainage are necessary for healthy growth
  • Botanical characteristics: Can grow up to 20m, Crown is thick and dark in color, Stem diameter can reach up to 60 cm, Bark is smooth and gray brown when young & cracks vertically and horizontally gradually darkening over time
  • Price: 500 Kshs

Macadamia nut

  • Environmental conditions: Thrives in full sun 6-8hrs a day, Well-draining slightly acidic to neutral soils, Warm, wet climates of between 61°F-77°F
  • Botanical characteristics: Can grow up to 18m high, Can spread out to about 15m wide, Leaves are shiny, leathery and about 20-30 cm in length, When mature each fruit cluster can contain up to 20 nuts
  • Price: 500 Kshs

African redwood tree

  • Environmental conditions: Thrives in full sunlight for optimal growth, Prefers well drained soils, Are resilient to climate changes
  • Botanical characteristics: Can grow up to 20m in height, Has a short trunk with thick branches and thick, peeling bark, Leaves are compound with 7-13 leaflets each about 10 cm long
  • Price: 1000 Kshs

Mahogany

  • Environmental conditions: Needs well draining soils rich in organic matter, Prefer abundant rainfall, high humidity and warm temperatures, Their presence contributes to overall health and biodiversity of tropical rainforests
  • Botanical characteristics: Compound and alternate pinnate leaves, dark green and shiny leaves on the upper surface and paler on the underside, small, fragrant, white or pinkish flowers, Fine wood, known for its straight fine grain and durability
  • Price: 1000 Kshs

Moringa tree

  • Environmental conditions: Needs a bright, sunny location for adequate sunlight, well draining and sandy soils, soil should be consistently moist, Temperatures should be between 77°F-95°F
  • Botanical characteristics: Can reach up to 9m tall, Has a corky gray bark hence a distinct appearance, bi or tripinnately compound leaves, oval-shaped leaflets, scented pea-like flowers with five stamens
  • Price: 900 Kshs
